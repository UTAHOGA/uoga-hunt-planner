Quick Legend
  • Live Utah Hunt Units: official hunt-unit boundary reference on the main map.
  • Land Layers: USFS, BLM, SITLA, state, and private-land context to help with access and planning.
  • Vetted Outfitters: U.O.G.A. trusted outfitter results tied to the selected hunt or unit.
How To Get To Vetted Outfitters
  1. Choose a hunt by number, unit, species, or filters in Find a Hunt.
  2. Select a hunt from the hunt list or click a hunt unit on the map.
  3. Leave Vetted Outfitters on in this U.O.G.A. service block.
  4. Review matches in the Vetted Outfitters panel on the right.
  5. Use Utah DWR Official if you want the state’s official hunt detail after that.
